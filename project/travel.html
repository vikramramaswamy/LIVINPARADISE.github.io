Birla Temple


The temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara, this stunning white marble structure is not only famous for its serene, calming atmosphere but also for its beautiful architecture that artfully mixes Rajasthani, Utkala and Dravidian styles together. The complex also houses a separate temple of Lord Bhudda. One can take the elevator up to the temple and enjoy beautiful views of the city and the Hussain Sagar Lake

Timings:7 am to 12 noon and 3 pm to 9 pm.

Mecca Masjid


The Mecca Masjid is one of the largest mosques in the world and among the oldest in India. In 1617 it was built by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutub Shah, it took close to 80 years for its construction to be completed. Mosque has stunning interiors as well with Belgian crystal chandeliers, beautifully designed five arches on each side, verse from Quran on arches and the doors. It is believed that the main hall alone can accommodate 10,000 worshippers at a time. Non-Muslims are not allowed inside the mosque, but you can admire other features like the serene pool and marble tombs that are the final resting spot for some of the former rulers.

Timings: 4.00 am – 9.30 pm

Calvary Temple



Calvary Temple is an evangelical non-denominational Christian megachurch in Hyderabad. In 2012, the Church began construction of an 18,000-seat indoor auditorium with galleries like an indoor stadium. It was built in 52 days and the venue was opened on 1 January 2013. The cavalry temple is visited by thousands of worshipers every Sunday that makes it be the biggest church in India.

Maula Ali Dargah



Built by Asif Jahis (Qutab Shahis) in the memory of Hazrat Ali who was the son-in-law of Prophet Muhammad. Located at the top of hillock Maula Ali, one must climb almost 400 steps to reach the Dargah. However, the long staircase going up to the hill make it less strenuous to climb. Many priests chanting hymns which strikingly makes the atmosphere calmer. Once at the Dargah, it is not just the religious significance that attracts you but also the view of the city from there. The mesmerizing view from the top is worth all the efforts. Timings: 5.30 am – 10.00 pm

Chilkur Balaji Temple



Chilkur is a small, picturesque village which is also a popular pilgrimage. It is in this sacred village where the famous Chilkur Balaji temple is built. On the banks of Osman Sagar Lake, close to the Vikarabad road, this temple attracts hordes of devotees from all over the country. Chilkur Balaji temple has a pleasing architecture too, boasting of being one of the oldest temples in Telangana. It is said that this temple was built more than half a millennium ago. Timings: 5.00 am 8.00 pm

stmarys-church



The St.Mary's Church at Secunderabad, Hyderabad is the oldest Roman Catholic Church in the city. This fascinating Church was built in Indo-Gothic style and was sanctified in 1850. The most striking features of this church are its curved arches and buttress. This unique church has its several side altars devoted to the saints. It has four bells brought all the way from Italy in the year 1901. he premises of the Church are a delight to one's eyes during Christmas when the entire area is lit with bright lights. People from all faiths visit the Church for its architectural beauty and is considered a vital landmark from decades in Secunderabad region. Timings: 8.00 am – 7.00pm
