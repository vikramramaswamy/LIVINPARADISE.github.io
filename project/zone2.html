1.Sky Zone, Hyderabad


Sky Zone Hyderabad is the first-ever Trampoline park in India that spread across 30,000 sq. ft offering world-class trampoline attractions to kids, and families. The center has loads of attractions and activities such as the Freestyle Jump, Foam Zone, Warped Wall and others. There are a few other activities such as Sky Joust, where you can jostle another person in a foam pit, or Ultimate Dodgeball, where you can play Dodgeball but, on a trampoline, allowing you to reach new heights with your jumps.
